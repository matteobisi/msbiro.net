The countdown to **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026** has officially entered its final stage! In just a few weeks, the global cloud-native community will descend upon the **RAI Amsterdam** (March 23–26), and I couldn't be more excited.

For **ReeVo**, this is a massive and strategic milestone. We are returning as a proud sponsor, and we are arriving in force. This isn't just a marketing trip; it’s a mission.

![ReeVo KubeCon 2026](reevo-kubecon-amsterdam.jpeg)

### Come Visit Us: Booth 893
If you are attending, please **come visit us at Booth 893**. Our delegation includes sales management, engineers, team leaders, and our marketing team. We have three main objectives:
1.  **Learn & Evolve:** Staying at the bleeding edge of the ecosystem.
2.  **Connect & Mentor:** Sharing our expertise in managed cloud and security services.
3.  **Strengthen Alliances:** Meeting our technical partners to further integrate our stack.

We are also thrilled to have the opportunity to spend these days with several of our **invited customers**, helping them navigate the complex cloud-native landscape directly from the heart of the community.

### My Personal Mission: Connections Beyond the Screen
On a personal level, KubeCon remains my favorite "networking marathon." It is the unique annual opportunity to meet in person a vast number of colleagues, contributors, and friends that I usually only see through a webcam.

Beyond the learning sessions, I’m particularly looking forward to:
*   **Human Connections:** Putting faces to names and shaking hands with the people who make this community great.
*   **The Team Factor:** Spending quality time with my ReeVo colleagues who live in different countries. We spend hundreds of hours in virtual meetings throughout the year, but nothing beats a face-to-face chat and a dinner together in a city as beautiful as Amsterdam.

Speaking of which, **Amsterdam** is one of my favorite European cities. The canals, the atmosphere, and the tech-forward vibe make it the perfect backdrop for this event.
### My Agenda: Sessions & Topics
I have officially started composing my personal agenda for the week, and it’s already looking packed! My week will actually start a day early: I was lucky enough to secure a ticket for **Open Source SecurityCon Europe** on March 23rd, the day before the official KubeCon kickoff. You can check out their [dedicated agenda here](https://colocatedeventseu2026.sched.com/overview/area/Open+Source+SecurityCon).

As always, **Security** remains my primary focus—specifically **Supply Chain Security**, **Runtime Observability**, and the latest **Kubernetes security features**.

However, this year there is a massive surge in **AI/ML** content that is impossible to ignore. I’m finding a lot of incredibly interesting sessions that explore the intersection of Cloud Native and Artificial Intelligence. If you are curious, you can check out the dedicated [AI + ML agenda here](https://kccnceu2026.sched.com/overview/type/AI+%2B+ML?iframe=no).

On a side note, March 25th happens to be my birthday! I can’t think of a better way to celebrate than being surrounded by the incredible energy of KubeCon. If you see me around the venue or at the booth, feel free to stop by and say hello—maybe we can toast with a coffee!
